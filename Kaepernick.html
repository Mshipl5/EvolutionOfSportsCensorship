Colin Kaepernick is a former NFL quarterback who used his platform to shed light on the injustices happening in America. On August 26, 2016, Colin Kaepernick decided to take a knee during the national anthem instead of standing with everyone else. This sparked a large controversy and outraged many people. Colin exercising his right to freedom of expression ultimately led to his indefinite suspension from the NFL. After Colin's suspension, many other athletes took a kneel during the anthem to show their solidarity and to let Kaepernick know that he is not alone. Kaepernick then sued NFL owners for collusion and recieved around $10 million from the NFL. Even though Kaepernick won his case, he has yet to be signed with an NFL team.
